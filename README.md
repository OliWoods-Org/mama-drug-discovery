> **1.7 billion people** are affected by neglected tropical diseases. Big pharma won't invest because the patients can't pay. AI can accelerate research at a fraction of the cost — making it economically viable to pursue treatments the market ignores.

## The Solution

6 agents accelerating the drug discovery pipeline from target identification to grant writing.

## Agents

**ProteinTargetFinder** — Identify druggable protein targets from genomic data
**BinderDesigner** — Design candidate molecules using AI structure prediction
**MoleculeScreener** — Virtual screening against compound libraries
**LiteratureScanner** — Systematic review of published research
**PartnerMatcher** — Connect researchers with labs, CROs, and funding
**GrantWriter** — Generate grant applications (Google.org AI for Science deadline: May 1, 2026)

## Safety

All research outputs require peer review before clinical application. This is a research acceleration tool, not a clinical decision tool.

## Data Sources

Ersilia Model Hub (open-source), PDB (Protein Data Bank), ChEMBL, UniProt, PubMed
